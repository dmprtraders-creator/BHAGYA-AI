### Swing Trade Setup (Daily)

#### Morning Routine (9:30 AM - 10 AM)
```
Step 1: Run Stock Analyzer
python daily_stock_analyzer.py

Step 2: Filter for SWING signals
- Look for RSI 30-35 (entering oversold)
- MACD about to cross above signal
- Price near support level
- Volume increasing on dips

Step 3: Identify setup
- Entry: Support level + positive signal
- Stop Loss: 5% below entry (mechanical)
- Target: 10-15% profit (2:1 risk-reward)

Step 4: Calculate position size
Position = ₹600 / (Stop loss %)
Example: ₹600 / 5% = ₹12,000 position
(If stock is ₹100, buy 120 shares)

Step 5: Place trade
- Set stop loss IMMEDIATELY
- Set profit target limit order
- NOTE the entry date/time
```

---

### SWING TRADE RULES (Mandatory)

**✅ DO:**
1. Risk only ₹600-900 per trade (1-3% of ₹30,000 account)
2. Always use stop loss
3. Wait for proper setup (don't force trades)
4. Hold 3-7 days minimum (patience wins)
5. Exit at target or stop loss (no exceptions)
6. Log every trade (date, entry, exit, P&L)

**❌ DON'T:**
1. Don't hold through news events
2. Don't average down (buy more at lower price)
3. Don't move stop loss further (discipline)
4. Don't overtrade (max 2-3 trades per week)
5. Don't trade with emotion (plan > feelings)

### Swing Trade Signals Decoder

From `daily_stock_analyzer.py` output:

#### 🟢 STRONG BUY Signal
```
RSI < 35 + MACD Bullish + Price above SMA20
Probability: ~60% win rate
Action: Enter swing trade
```

#### 🟡 WEAK BUY Signal
```
Only 1-2 indicators bullish
Probability: ~50% win rate
Action: Wait for better setup / reduce position size
```

#### 🔴 SELL Signal (Exit)
```
RSI > 70 OR MACD bearish OR break support
Action: Close trade immediately
```

## ⚠️ RISK MANAGEMENT (Critical)

### Position Sizing Formula
```
Risk per Trade = 1-3% of Account

Example (₹30,000 swing account):
- 1% risk = ₹300 per trade
- 3% risk = ₹900 per trade

If stop loss is 5%:
Position Size = ₹900 / 5% = ₹18,000 (180 shares at ₹100)
```

### Account Loss Limits
```
If you lose 20% in swing trading → STOP
(From ₹30,000 → ₹24,000 = Stop & review strategy)

Maximum monthly loss: 10% (₹3,000)
If reached → No more trading that month
```

### Red Flags (Don't Trade These)
```
❌ Stock down 40%+ in one day
❌ Debt-to-Equity > 3
❌ Revenue declining 3+ quarters
❌ Key people leaving
❌ Auditor qualified opinion
❌ Stock at 10-year low
❌ Volume extremely low
```

## 💬 Psychological Tips

### For Quick Money (Swing Trading):
1. **Accept losses** - Even 50% win rate makes money (2:1 reward:risk)
2. **Don't revenge trade** - Losses happen, move to next trade
3. **Follow your plan** - Rules > Emotions
4. **Celebrate small wins** - ₹300 profit is still profit
5. **Track everything** - Identify patterns in losses

### For Long-Term (Buying & Holding):
1. **Ignore daily noise** - 20-30% swings are normal
2. **Check quarterly, not daily** - Reduces anxiety
3. **Reinvest dividends** - Compound growth is your friend
4. **Add on dips** - Buy when everyone else is scared
5. **Think in years, not months** - Patience beats rushing
